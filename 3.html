Khazix

The best ad assasin Khazix can one shot a carry while escaping with either , or . His slipperiness and massive damage is what makes him so good.

The Specifics

Khazix passive provides bonus damage to enemies when he is not on vision and in combination with his Q which provides 110 % bonus damage to isolated targets allows him to delete carries that are mispositioned. His upgraded R allows Khazix to be invisble for 2 seconds and gives him 40 % movement speed so even if a carry is positioned properly he can easily sneak up on them. Khazix also provides a lot of utility in the form of his upgraded W since it covers the entire width of a lane and slows non isolated enemies by 60 % and isolated ones by 90%.
